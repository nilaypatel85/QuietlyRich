Why No International in Any 401(k)

Foreign govts withhold ~0.6% of international ETF positions. In taxable you recover it via Foreign Tax Credit. Inside any 401(k) that credit is permanently lost. VXUS stays in taxable. Solo 401(k) uses VTV — same 7.5% fwd return, zero foreign drag.
